What we do

We offer an outsourcing service to generate interlinear and / or online fares, through a software, that uses a customizable methodology, guaranteesing positive revenue to airlines.

 

Read More

Who We are

With the pandemic caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus and the safety measures taken at the global level, the air transport of passengers was affected by the decrease in flight frequencies, cancellation of routes and staff reduction generating greater dependency of the interline product and the need to assign competitive prices in online markets.

This situation motivated a group of Panamanian engineers to establish the company FixAlytics Corp., with the purpose of offering an automated software to perform air transport fare analysis with options to position themselves with better alternatives for recovery and revenue generation.

Our Solution provides:

Increase Revenue

Analyzing, processing and generating fares to all networks, in minutes, through a software that guarantees positive revenues.

Eliminate Revenue Losses and Fares Publication Errors identifying and transforming

Fares with negative revenue into positive revenue, and errors that affect Revenue Management Systems.

Optimize Resources

Through outsourcing services, the fares are monitored and generated, allowing the possibility for airline's pricing staff to be redirected to strategic areas of the company.

What we use to provide the service?

A software named FAAST (Fares Automated Analysis Software for Transport) that carries out the following:
  • Analyze and Process thousands of fares in minutes
  • Create new fares if they donÂ´t exist
  • Transform fares that it identifies with negative revenue to turn them into positive ones
  • Identify and fix fare errors
  • Generate a File of fares ready to be published
  • Generate reports of actions taken

Mission

FixAlytics Corp., provides automated and personalized services to perform fare analysis of air, maritime and land transport (FAAST) worldwide, which allows companies to be more competitive and increase income in interline and online markets.

Vision

Position ourselves as a leading company in automated and personalized Technological Solutions for air, maritime and land transport companies worldwide, generating fares, seeking to increase users in their route network, with greater profitability and social function.
